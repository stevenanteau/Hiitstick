Your Design Team

You can't design a life alone. A small team of people who care about you — and will tell you the truth kindly — makes the whole thing lighter and better. Three to five is plenty.

Who's on a team?

SupportersThey cheer you on and care about your progress.
PlayersActive participants in your projects and plans.
IntimatesClose family or friends your design will affect.
MentorsThey offer counsel — helping you figure out what you think.

Counsel, not advice

A good mentor gives counsel (“tell me more — what are you weighing?”), not advice (“here's what I'd do”). If you hear “If I were you…”, gently steer back to your own thinking. You want their questions and stories, not their answers.
