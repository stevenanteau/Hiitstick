Your life isn't a problem to be solved — it's a design problem to be explored. There's no single right answer; there are many good designs for your life, and you build your way forward through small experiments. These exercises (adapted from Designing Your Life) help you get curious and get unstuck.

The five design mindsets

Bring these along to every activity.

🔍
CuriosityEverything is interesting — ask questions and explore.
🔨
Bias to actionBuild your way forward; try things rather than only thinking.
🔄
ReframingChange your perspective to get unstuck.
🌱
AwarenessKnow it's a process — embrace the journey.
🤝
Radical collaborationAsk for help; you can't do this alone.

A few reframes to carry

Old belief on the left, a more useful one on the right.

I'm stuck.
→
I'm never stuck — I can always generate more ideas.
I need to find the one right idea.
→
I need many ideas to explore possibilities.
I should know where I'm going.
→
I can tell if I'm going in the right direction.
It's too late.
→
It's never too late to design a life you love.
Work isn't supposed to be enjoyable.
→
Enjoyment is a guide to finding the right work.
